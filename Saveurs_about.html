Saveurs

A handful of French restaurants in Taipei that serves genuine French fare and offers diners the chance to enjoy a selection of home-style cooking from the Perigueux region of France, an area renowned for its truffles, duck, goose and cepes mushrooms. In addition, its beef Bourgogne is one of the highlighted and outstanding dishes on the menu. Besides these, Saveurs' minimalist design blends the ambience of fine dining with the coziness of a small family-run restaurant.
